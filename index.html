Privacy Policy – Remindly

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

Remindly is a smart note and reminder application designed to work fully offline. Your privacy is important to us.

2. Data Collection

Remindly does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No account is required
  • No personal information is transmitted
  • No data is sent to external servers

3. Local Data Storage

All data created within the app (notes, reminders, boards, settings) is stored locally on your device.

This data never leaves your device unless you manually export it.

4. Permissions

Remindly may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: to alert you about reminders
  • Exact Alarm: to trigger reminders at precise times
  • Microphone: to trigger the microphone while speaking in reminders.

These permissions are used only for core app functionality and not for tracking.

5. Ads

The free version of Remindly may display advertisements. These ads may be provided by third-party services (such as Google AdMob), which may collect anonymous usage data.

Users can remove ads through a one-time premium purchase.

6. Data Sharing

Remindly does not share any user data with third parties.

7. Security

Since all data is stored locally, you are responsible for your device’s security.

8. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected on this page.
